About Jade

Jade is a playful and intelligent Border Collie who has a special fascination with bubbles. Born and raised on a farm in the countryside, Jade has always had a love for the outdoors and enjoys spending her days running and playing in the open fields. Her boundless energy and inquisitive nature make her a joy to be around.

One day, while playing in the backyard, Jade discovered bubbles for the first time. From that moment on, she was hooked. She would chase after them with excitement, leaping and bounding in the air as she tried to catch them with her paws. Her enthusiasm for bubbles quickly became her favorite pastime.

Aside from her love for bubbles, Jade is also known for her loyalty and affection towards her family. She forms strong bonds with those around her and is always eager to please. Whether it's going for long walks, playing fetch, or simply cuddling on the couch, Jade brings endless joy and happiness to those who know her.

Care

Border Collies like Jade require regular exercise and mental stimulation to thrive. They are highly intelligent and need activities that challenge their minds, such as obedience training, agility courses, and interactive games. Additionally, they need plenty of space to run and play, so a large backyard or access to open areas is ideal.

Proper socialization is also important for Border Collies to prevent behavioral issues. Early training and exposure to different environments and people will help them develop into well-rounded and confident dogs. Regular grooming, including brushing their coat and keeping their nails trimmed, is necessary to keep them looking and feeling their best.
